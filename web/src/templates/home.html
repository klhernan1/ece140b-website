The Art of Product Engineering

This website designed by myself, Karen Hernandez, a finishing undergraduate student at UCSD majoring in Electrical Engineering with emphasis in machine learning and data science.



Motivation

The goal of this space is to demonstrate my experience writting full-stack explore some projects that I have contributed to.


The Art of product engineering is a two-term course offered at UCSD, naimly as ECE 140A and ECE 140B. The goal is to build on a solid foundation of electrical and computer engineer skills, this course strives to broaden student skills in software, full-stack engineering, and concrete understanding of methods related to the realistic development of a commercial product. Students will research, design, and develop an IOT device to serve an emerging market.
