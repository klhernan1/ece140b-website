Sources of Revenue Streams

Banglet is a service purchased by event or venue organnizers. The amount of sell depends on the scale of the even.The project managers during large events or organizer will be directly working with us to set up the neccessary requirements for the event. There are two options, one time deal which includes the service for one even, or subscription for multiple events.

Channels of Revenue

There is only one main channel of revenue which is direct sale with the organizers.

Expected Revenue Income

Annual income expected is based on MLB and NFL games during one year average based on past 3 years. 243 MLB games/events which offers more than 29 milion dollars. and 24 NFL games and events which offers almost 33 milion dollars in revenue for the total of 62 million dollars.
